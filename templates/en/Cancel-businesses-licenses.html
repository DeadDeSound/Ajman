Service Description

it is a legal and administrative action to be done by the owner / license holders (establishment or the like) with intent to cancel the facility license (the activity) finally ..

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

employee / dealer personnel / dealer companies / establishments

Documents required for the service

  • The original license or Copy of it.
  • NOC from the Ministry of Labor.
  • Cancel residence or bring proof that the visa is not on the canceled license.

Steps of service

  • Fill out the cancellation form
  • Provide the required documents
  • Pay the fines, if any,
  • The provision of NOC from the Ministry of Labor
  • Cancel residence or bring proof that the visa is not on the canceled license Required approvals and attestations from external entities

Required approvals and attestations from external entities

  • Company liquidation letter attested by court.

Forms for requesting the service

form : Cancel EA-5-1-F4

The concerned department to provide the service

Economic licenses section
