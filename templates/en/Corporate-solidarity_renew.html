Joint ventures

Service Description

renewal of licenses for joint venture companies

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / organizations

Documents required for the service

  • Copy of the identity card for all stakeholders except visitors or GCC nationals.
  • Copy of valid passport for license holders .
  • The original license or Copy of it in case of loss.
  • Inspection card.
  • Copy of the identity card for all stakeholders except visitors or GCC nationals.
  • Copy of the lease contract attestation or Copy of the certificate of ownership of the property in the event that the property is in the name of the license holder.
  • Copy of a lease contract or ownership of warehouses, if any.
  • Copy of the leases contracts .

Steps to provide service

  • Fill in the license application form
  • Attach the required documents
  • Get approval from the control and inspection section
  • The provision of external approvals
  • Approval of documents validity from consulting counter.
  • Payment Fees
  • Receipt of the license

Required approvals and attestations from external entities

  • Approval from other government agencies for the renewal, as stipulated in the list of foreign approvals based on activity..

Forms for requesting the service

license application EA-1-1-F1

The concerned department to provide the service

Economic licenses section
