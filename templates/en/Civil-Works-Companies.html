Service Description

obtaining a license to establish civil works company.

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / organizations

Documents required for obtaining new licenses in general

  • Copy of the identity card and family book for locals (khulasat Quaid).
  • Copy of the passport of the license owners.
  • Complete the registration and the commercial license form, available at the website of the department and in the service centers.
  • Copy of the passport page of residence or a visit visa for non-GCC citizens.
  • No objection letter from the sponsor for exercising commercial activity â€“applicable for non-GCC citizens.
  • No objection letter to perform trading activities from the department of courts for those under 21 years old.
  • Approval of other government agencies on the required activity in case it is needed.

    Note: After obtaining the initial approval and reserving the trade name: receipt of the initial approval and all documents previously submitted the following documents must be provided : -

  • Copy of the certificate for attesting tenancy contract issued by the Department of Municipality and Planning .
  • Memorandum of Association duly attested applicable to all companies .
  • Services agent appointment contract duly attested applicable for professional and civil companies 100% owned by residents who are not GCC nationals which require a local services agent from the United Arab Emirates.
  • Any other external approvals required for the activity to be practiced.

Documents required for the service

  • Copy of the passport + Family Book + Emirates ID.
  • Copy of the family book for locals
  • Copy of the identity card and family book for locals
  • Copy of a valid lease contract or copy of ownership or valid site map in the event that the property is in the name of the license holder.
  • Obtain initial approval and reserve for trade name : initial approval receipt .
  • NOC letter from the department of residency and foreigners affairs for expatriate partners .
  • Educational degrees and experiences certificates to obtain consulting licenses of various types plus warehouse lease contract, if any.
  • Site map of shop + lease contract (Please confirm the suitability of the site for the activity by inspection section before leasing ).
  • A letter from the court to authorize exercising trade for those who are less than 21 years old.
  • Memorandum of Association of the civil works company.
  • Copy of the passport for partners
  • Passport copy of the license holder + residence visa (for non-citizens).
  • Valid lease contract attested by municipality.
  • Services agent contract .
  • NOC certified letter from the sponsor (non-citizens).

Steps to provide service

  • fill in the license application form
  • Attach the required documents
  • Get approval from the control and inspection section
  • Reserve for trade name
  • The provision of external approvals
  • Review consulting to approve validity of documents
  • Payment of fees
  • Receipt of the license

Approvals and attestations required from external entities

  • Approval of government agencies by activity.
  • Approval of the Ames company (Idustrial areas and part of Nuaimiya).
  • approval of civil defense.

Forms for requesting the service

license application EA-1-1-F1
