A limited liability company

Service Description

obtaining a license to establish a limited liability company

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / organizations

Documents required for obtaining new licenses in general

  • Copy of the identity card and family book for locals (khulasat Quaid).
  • Copy of the passport of the license owners.
  • Complete the registration and the commercial license form, available at the website of the department and in the service centers.
  • Copy of the passport page of residence or a visit visa for non-GCC citizens.
  • No objection letter from the sponsor for exercising commercial activity â€“applicable for non-GCC citizens.
  • No objection letter to perform trading activities from the department of courts for those under 21 years old.
  • Approval of other government agencies on the required activity in case it is needed.

    Note: After obtaining the initial approval and reserving the trade name: receipt of the initial approval and all documents previously submitted the following documents must be provided : -

  • Copy of the certificate for attesting tenancy contract issued by the Department of Municipality and Planning .
  • Memorandum of Association duly attested applicable to all companies .
  • Services agent appointment contract duly attested applicable for professional and civil companies 100% owned by residents who are not GCC nationals which require a local services agent from the United Arab Emirates.
  • Any other external approvals required for the activity to be practiced.

Documents required for the service

  • Copy of valid identity card for locals .
  • Copy of the passport of local or GCC owner's and the same with residence page for expatriate owners.
  • NOC from the previous company from the concerned Chamber of Commerce.
  • If the investor desire to transfer residence he must obtain the prior approval of the passports department , if transfer of residence is not required he must provide the undertaking form approved by the department.
  • Provide the memorandum of association certified by a notary public and the receipt e-Dirham from the Ministry of Economy .
  • Copy of the passport of the license holders .
  • Copy of a valid attested lease contract or copy the ownership deed of the property.
  • If the partnership is between GCC or local citizens or between themselves shares percentages not required if there is a resident partner the shares of the local partner must not be less than 51% .
  • Complete the registration and the commercial license form , available at the website of the department and in the service centers.
  • Copy of the residence page or visit visa for non GCC citizens.
  • NOC letter from the sponsor for non GCC residents to engage in commercial activity.
  • NOC letter from the department of residency and foreigners affairs for expatriate partners .
  • A letter from the court to exercise trade for any person less than 21 years old.
  • Educational degrees and practical experience for obtaining consulting licenses of various kinds.
  • Board of Directors' resolution to enter into a new partnership (in the event that the partner is a company registered either within the country or abroad, that resolution shall be certified by the embassies of the UAE and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and legally translated into Arabic.
  • NOC letter from the former sponsor of partners for non-citizens approved by the Chamber of Commerce or notary public .
  • Plans for the shop + lease contract or title deed + warehouse lease contract if applicable (please refer to the inspection department to check the suitability of the site to exercise the required activity before leasing).

Steps to be followed

  • Fill in the license application form
  • Attach the required documents
  • Get approval from the control and inspection section
  • Reserve for trade name
  • The provision of external approvals
  • Approve validity of documents from the consulting counter
  • Payment of fees
  • Receipt of the license

Approvals and attestations required from other entities

  • Attest the lease contract from the municipality.
  • Approval of civil defense.
  • Ames company approval (for industrial areas and part of Nuaimiya)
  • Approval of government agencies based on activity type ( list attached ).
