Service Description

renewal of licenses for branches of companies of other emirate

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / establishments

Documents required for the service

  • Copy of the parent companyâ€™s valid license.
  • Copy of the identity card for all stakeholders except the visitor and the GCC nationals.
  • Copy of valid passport of the license holders .
  • Copy of the lease contract attestation or copy the certificate of ownership of the property in the event that the property is in the name of the license holder.
  • Copy of a lease or ownership of warehouses, if any.

Steps of service

  • Fill in the license application form
  • Attach the required documents
  • Get approval from the control and inspection management
  • The provision of external approvals
  • Approve validity of documents from the consulting counter
  • Payment Fees
  • Receipt of the license

Required approvals and attestations from external entities

  • Approval from other government agencies for the renewal, as stipulated in the list of foreign approvals based on activity

Forms for requesting the service
