Sole proprietorships

Service Description

renewal of of sole proprietorship licenses

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / establishments

Documents required for the service

  • Copy of a valid identity card for the local owner
  • Copy of the passport of the local or GCC owner
  • Copy of the lease contract attestation certificate , or Copy of the certificate of ownership of the property in the event that the property is in the name of the license holder

Steps to provide service

  • Filling the license application form
  • Attach the required documents
  • Get approval from the control and inspection section
  • The provision of external approvals
  • Approve validity of documents from the consulting counter
  • Payment of fees
  • Receipt of the license

Required approvals and attestations from external entities

  • Approval from other government agencies for the renewal, as stipulated in the list of foreign approvals based on activity.

Forms for requesting the service

license application EA-1-1-F1

The concerned department to provide the service

Economic licenses section
