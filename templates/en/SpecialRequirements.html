Special conditions for exercising economic activities

Real estate offices

  • Obtaining the approval of Land and Property Department.
  • Office space of not less than 60 square meters.
  • Office to be located on the front of (main street or within an offices building).

Real estate development offices

  • Obtaining the approval of the Real Estate Regulatory Department.
  • Not less office space from 100 square meters.
  • Office to be located on the front of main street or within an offices building

Real estate development offices

  • Obtaining the approval of the Real Estate Regulatory Department.
  • Not less office space from 100 square meters.

Construction contracting and engineering consultancy offices

  • Obtaining the approval of Municipality and Planning Department (Engineering Affairs Sector).
  • Office area not less than 100 square meters.

Stores that sell gas cylinders

  • Get the approval from Civil Defense Department.
  • Office space of not less than 60 square meters.
  • The provision of a special warehouse for cylinders or contracting with filling factories and provide copy of the letter from the factory.
  • Do exercise filling activity in the shop or the warehouse filling must be in the filling plant site only.
  • Importing cylinders between emirates is prohibited.
  • Commitment to the price list published from the Ministry of Economy.

The activities of Garages & car workshops, general maintenance(Dye & plumbing-mechanic repair-vehicle structure repair)

  • Facilities are to licensed in the industrial areas only.
  • Shop area of not less than 100 square meters per activity also it entails providing the same space if you add another activity.
  • Not to exercise activity outside the boundary of the shop.
  • Provide a fenced area or shed area not less than 300 square meters for the activities of public workshops for car maintenance.
  • Compliance with activities specified by the licensing and to refer to the department in the case of the desire to add other activities.

Tire trade and repair

  • Facilities are to licensed in the industrial areas only.
  • Shop area of not less than 100 square meters per activity also it entails providing double the space to carry both activities.
  • In the case of using a hydraulic crane in the shop the height of the ceiling should be considered.
  • Not to exercise activity outside the boundary of the shop

Trading in new auto spare parts, heavy machines and equipment

  • Activity is allowed to operate in industrial areas only.
  • Area of not less than 500 square meters or the provision of spare parts warehouse and in the case of contracting with car agencies copy of the contract is a must;
  • Not to exercise the activity of replace and repair unless a place is provided for the practice of the additional activity and after the approval of the department.
  • Do not put subjects outside the shop.

Conditions for industrial workshops (blacksmithing, welding and aluminum and glass and kitchen activities)

  • Activity is allowed to operate in industrial areas only.
  • Performing work outside the boundary of the workshop is prevented and not store items outside the boundary of the facility.
  • For repair of heavy equipment activities the area shall not be less than 500 square meters.
  • Adhere to issue receipt of delivery to the consumer and a commitment to deliver orders on time and guarantee
