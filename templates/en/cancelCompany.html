LOCATION OF SERVICE PROVISION

Available on all Ajman DED Customer Service Center

SERVICE PROVISION TIME:

During Office Working Hours

SERVICE BENEFICIARY

Employee | Individuals liaison officer | Corporate | liaison officer

The Required Documents:

  • Filling in the application form.
  • The original copy of license, or photocopy (if lost)
  • Identity documents for the assignee

Procedures

  • Submitting the request for Authentication in the presence of concerned party.
  • Referring to Control & Inspection Division + Referring to Drainage Department
  • Collecting fees, then transferring the transaction to Notary Public
  • Referring to other governmental authorities (Chamber of Commerce + Ministry of Labor)
  • Referring to the Notary Public for publishing and after 15 days, the certificate to be printed and delivered to customer
