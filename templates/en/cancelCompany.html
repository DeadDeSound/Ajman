LOCATION OF SERVICE PROVISION

Available on all Ajman DED Customer Service Center

SERVICE PROVISION TIME:

During Office Working Hours

SERVICE BENEFICIARY

Employee | Individuals liaison officer | Corporate | liaison officer

The Required Documents:

1. Filling in the application form.
2. The original copy of license, or photocopy (if lost)
3. Identity documents for the assignee

Procedures

1. Submitting the request for Authentication in the presence of concerned party.
2. Referring to Control & Inspection Division + Referring to Drainage Department
3. Collecting fees, then transferring the transaction to Notary Public
4. Referring to other governmental authorities (Chamber of Commerce + Ministry of Labor)
5. Referring to the Notary Public for publishing and after 15 days, the certificate to be printed and delivered to customer
