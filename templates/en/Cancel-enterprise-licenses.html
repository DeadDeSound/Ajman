Service Description

it is a legal and administrative action to be done by the owner / license holders (establishment or the like) with intent to cancel the facility license (the activity) finally ..

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

employee / dealer personnel / dealer companies / establishments

Documents required for the service

• Filling the application
• The original license or Copy of it in the case of loss
• Documentary papers for the owners of the license

Steps of service

1. The submission of the application for the adoption in the presence of stakeholders
2. Review the inspection and review section
3. Collection of fees
4. Review other government departments (Chamber of Commerce + labor office )
5. Publication in the newspaper, and after 15 days of publication of the announcement the cancelling certificate to issued for the client

Forms for requesting the service

form : Cancel EA-5-1-F4

The concerned department to provide the service

cancellation and waiver section
