Service Description

renewal of licenses for civil works companies

Time of service

During the official working hours of service centers

The beneficiary of the service

companies / establishments

Documents required for the service

• Valid copy of the identity card of the local owner
• Copy of the passport of the investor
• The original license or Copy of it in case of loss.
• Inspection card.
• Copy of the leas contract attestation or copy of the certificate of ownership of the property in the event that the property is in the name of the license holder.
• Copy of a lease or ownership of warehouses, if any.

Steps of service

1. Fill in the license application form
2. Attach the required documents
3. Get approval from the control and inspection management
4. The provision of external approvals
5. Approve documents from the consulting counter
6. Payment Fees
7. Receipt of the license

Required approvals and attestations from external entities

• Approval from other government agencies for the renewal, as stipulated in the list of foreign approvals based on activity.

Forms for requesting the service

license application EA-1-1-F1

The concerned department to provide the service

Economic licenses section
